# Silicon_Foundation v0.1.0

A foundation for silicon across refining purity, device yield/thermal constraints, and conservative claim screening.

**vs design stack:** `Fabless` and `foundry.implementation.tick` are **not** fab process physics. For where wafer feedstock, gases, dopants, and metals live in `3_meterial`, see the [semiconductor materials bridge](../docs/SEMICONDUCTOR_MATERIALS_BRIDGE_EN.md). Photoresist/CMP consumables: `Photolithography_CMP_Foundation` (not EDA).
