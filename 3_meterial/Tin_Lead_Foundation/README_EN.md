# Tin_Lead_Foundation v0.1.0

Foundational layer pairing tin (Sn) and lead (Pb) for **soldering, RoHS constraints, lead exposure pathways, and tin-whisker risk** in electronics interconnects.
Screening down-weights claims that whiskers are fully eliminated or that lead is harmless without controls.
